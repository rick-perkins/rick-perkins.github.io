About EcoStar
EcoStar is a well established and well known family run business within the Hampshire area.

Within EcoStar there is are over 30 years of electrical contracting experience including previous work for Channel4 and the BBC on well known TV shows, high street chains, office referbs and insurance work nationwide to Europe amongst other things.
Prior to the setup of the EcoStar venture a successful 22 year joint ownership was had with Five Star.

EcoStar's view is to encourage eco friendly, low energy lighting and heating with their extensive knowledge in solar electrical, heat pumps and air or ground source energy heating.

EcoStar is fully compliant to the latest building and electrical regulations and are members of NICEIC and Trust Mark.
