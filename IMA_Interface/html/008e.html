Dry Alarm

This section allows you to add alarm codes, when the alarm is active this will trigger the volt free normally open contacts to close.

Alarm 1 is linked to J12 pin 9 SW4-A and pin 10 SW4-B
Alarm 2 is linked to J12 pin 7 SW3-A and pin 8 SW3-B

The tick box allows this function to be disabled when its unticked.
