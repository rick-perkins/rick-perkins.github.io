Connections to the DAC's

RX is from the DAC output on the tuner card and TX goes directly to the MUX panel to terminate with the ships cable.
Click here to add the coax cables.

Next is to connect up the serial connection.
This is for arbitrator switching, RX lock and GPS. This goes to the TMS.
(Note: The TX mute is not done by the DAC it's controlled by the arbitrator only)
Click here to add the serial modem cable

Next is to connect the Ethernet cables.This is for the web interface, ROAM or openAMIP if used.
Click here to add the Ethernet cables

For DAC B, connect in the same way but use the B ports instead on the arbitrator.

16 MUST be added to both DAC system types and having JP4 installed on the TMS may stop the RX lock from working when not using openAMIP.
