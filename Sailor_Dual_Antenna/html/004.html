Blockage Zones

The blockage zones are recommened to be defined in 3 ways.
  • Actual blocking zones on the vessel (No TX)
  • Switching blocking zones (TX Allowed)
  • Personal saftey zones (No TX)
The blockage zones will need to be set for both the Master and Slave ACU's.
Programmed blockage will switch the antennas within milliseconds
Unexpected blockage uses a 2 minutie timer before the antenna will switch to the other antenna.
