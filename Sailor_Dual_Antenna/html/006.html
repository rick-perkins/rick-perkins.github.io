Switching

Using the links below to connect up the dual antenna system.

Click to put the Master ACU into blockage or an error state. Click to take the Master ACU out of blockage or error state.

Click to put the Slave ACU into blockage or an error state. Click to take the Slave ACU out of blockage or error state.

Master antenna goes into blockage. The VIM will remove the 10hz to the BUC resulting in the BUC being muted and mutes the RX.
Master antenna is out of blockage and the Slave antenna is active. The Master antenna will go in to Generic mode with the BUC and RX muted.
Slave antenna goes into blockage. The VIM will remove the 10hz to the BUC resulting in the BUC being muted and mutes the RX.
Slave antenna is out of blockage and the Master antenna is active. The Slave antenna will go in to Generic mode with the BUC and RX muted.
Both antennas are blockage. The VIM will remove the 10hz to the BUC resulting in the BUC being muted and mutes the RX.
