RF and Data Connections

Using the links below to connect up the dual antenna system.

Click to add RF cables to the Modem.
Connect up the RX and TX coax cables to the side of the splitter/combiner with one F-connector on it.

Click to add the modem Ethernet cable.
Connect up the Ethernet cable between the modem Ethernet port and Ethernet port 1 on the Master ACU.

Click to add the modem Serial cable.
Connect up the serial cable between the modem console port and the RS232 port on the Master ACU if needed.

Click to add RF cables to the ACU's
Connect up the RX and TX coax cables to the splitter/combiner with ACU's RX and TX F-connector.

Click to add the Ethernet cable between the ACU's
Link Ethernet port 2 on the master ACU to Ethernet port 2 on the slave ACU.
This is to allow the Master and Slave ACU's to comunicate with each other.
