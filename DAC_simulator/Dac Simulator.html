LIGHTS
Flowchart Map
Tracking - (Green LED)
  • ON indicates that the ACU has identified and is activly tracking the desired satelite to optimize the signal level (AGC).
  • BLINKING indicates that the ACU is in search delay or is analyzing a satellite signal.
  • OFF indicates that Tracking is OFF or the antenna is in search or target mode.
Searching - (Yellow LED)
  • ON indicates that the ACU is activley searching for your satellite signal.
  • OFF indicates that SEARCH is OFF.

Target - (Yellow LED)

  • ON indicates that the antenna is TARGETING (driving) to the specified Azimuth and/ or Elevation position(s).
  • ON also indicates UNWRAP in limited azimuth antennas (04 series).
Power - (Green LED)
  • ON indicates that the Antenna Control Unit is energized.
Initializing - (Green LED)
  • ON indicates that the Antenna is initializing. Initialization of the antenna will take approximately two minutes.
Error - (Red LED)
  • ON inidicates that one, or more, discrete system errors have occurred. Refer to Status - Error Code information menu to determine which error(s) have occurred.
  • OFF indicates that no errors have occurred.
NEXT BUTTON

Press NEXT to cycle though the four main menus:Ship, Satellite, Antenna and Status (refer to the Operation Flowcharts).

TRACK BUTTON

Press TRACK key to toggle the state of Tracking ON/OFF. AGC must be above threshold to enable tracking. If AGC is not above threshold, the system wil enter into a search delay.

Revision Screen
LEFT & RIGHT ARROWS

In any sub-menu, pressing the LEFT or RIGHT arrow enters editing mode and brings up a cursor in the display. When the cursor is under a character, it is selected and can be changed (see UP/DOWN arrow below).
When the Antenna main menu is displayed, pressing the LEFT arrow moves the antenna left (CCW or down in azimuth).Pressing the RIGHT arrow to move the antenna right (CW or up in azimuth).

SETUP BUTTONS

Press and release BOTH the LEFT and RIGHT arrow buttons to access the save parameters window. Press and Hold for 6 seconds to access the setup parameters.
Note: For the purposes of the Simulator, simply click the circle located in the center of the arrow keys.

UP & DOWN ARROWS

Pressing the UP or DOWN arrows cycles Up and Down though the sub-menus. When a sub-menu item is being edited, use the UP or DOWN to increment or decrement the selected character. This steps the selected entry one increment per sequential entry when pressed and held.
When the Antenna main menu is displayed, press UP arrow to move the antenna up in elevation or the DOWN arrow to move the antenna down in elevation.

RESET BUTTON

Press RESET to reset all the processors in the Antenna Control Unit. It does NOT reset the antenna.

POWER BUTTON

Switch to ON position to apply AC power to the ACU.(USAT and Series 04 antennas also applies power to the above decks equipment).

ENTER BUTTON

At any main menu level, press ENTER to access the sub-menu items.
When viewing one of the sub-menus pressing the ENTER key will step down though the sub-menu items, like the DOWN arrow does.
When editing, the cursor is visible under a character in a sub-menu (whether it has been changed or not), press ENTER to execute the present value, and return to display mode for that sub-menu. This does NOT save the new value to the NVRAM.
