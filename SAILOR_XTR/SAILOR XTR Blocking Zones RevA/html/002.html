Configuration

  1. Log in to the ACU.
  2. Go to settings.
  3. Click on blocking zones.
  4. Enter the correct azimuth and elevation angles.
  5. Tick No TX if the blocking zone is to mute the TX.
  6. Tick active to make the blocking zone active.
  7. Click apply to save the information.
  • The blocking zones will be displayed on the interactive illustration.
  • Blocking zones are displayed as a grey colour.
  • No TX zones are shown as a red colour.
  • The antenna illustration shows the pointing direction for the antenna.
