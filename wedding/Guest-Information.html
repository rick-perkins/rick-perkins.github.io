Black Tie optional...
You will have noticed on your invitation that we have a black tie dress code in place.
For gentlemen this would indicate that traditional tuxedos are preferred but any black/dark suit will be appropriate should you wish to keep things simple.
For ladies this means full evening dress would be appropriate.

We wish all our guests to attend worry free and in comfort so please do not feel obliged to conform to the dress code if it makes your attendance difficult. Please know that your presence is what is most important to us.
Children
Please be understanding that we have decided to keep our guest list small and intimate. Due to venue restrictions we have decided not to include children as guests at our wedding.
Gifts
We understand that attending weddings can be expensive and so the giving of a wedding gift is not neccessary.
Should you wish to contribute to our Familymoon/first ever holiday together, a small donation would be very much welcomed.
