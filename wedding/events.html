Local Public Transport Options
There is a free shuttle bus from the Red Jet passenger ferry terminal to Southampton Central Train Station. It is a 7min walk from the station to The Vestry. Cross to the other side of the station via the pedestrian bridge and turn left on Blechynden Terrace. Take the first right onto Wyndham Place and once at the end of the road take another right. The Vestry is on the left hand side of the road, directly opposite the theatre.


There is very limited parking at The Vestry so we recommend that guests use public transport where possible or leave vehicles at their place of accommodation. There are several public carparks nearby (parking restrictions and parking charges will apply).
